Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Automation and how can it help my business?

AI Automation replaces repetitive manual tasks with intelligent systems that work 24/7. From document processing and email classification to data entry and workflow orchestration, our solutions typically save businesses 40+ hours per week while improving accuracy to 99%.

What does an External CAO/CAIO do?

An External Chief AI Officer (CAIO) or Chief Automation Officer (CAO) provides executive-level AI strategy without the full-time cost. We embed with your team to evaluate AI opportunities, select the right technologies, manage vendors, and build internal capabilities — ensuring your AI investments deliver real ROI.

Are SAITS solutions GDPR compliant?

Yes, 100%. All our solutions are built with GDPR and Swiss data protection (DSG) compliance as a core requirement. We use European data hosting, implement data minimization principles, and provide full audit trails.

How long does it take to implement an AI automation solution?

Typical deployments take 4 weeks from discovery to initial deployment. We follow a structured 5-step process: Discovery, Analysis, Design, Build, and ongoing Support to ensure minimal disruption to your operations.

What industries do you serve?

We serve businesses across all industries worldwide, with deep expertise in real estate, financial services, construction, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, logistics, education, automotive, and the public sector.
