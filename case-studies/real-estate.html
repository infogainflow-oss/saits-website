The Challenge

A leading real estate firm managing over 3,000 residential and commercial properties faced significant delays in their property valuation process. Each valuation required manual analysis of comparable sales, market trends, property features, and location data.

Key challenges included:

  • Time-intensive process: Each valuation took 2-3 days of analyst time
  • Data silos: Information scattered across multiple systems and spreadsheets
  • Inconsistent methodology: Different analysts produced varying valuations for similar properties
  • Market delays: Properties sat unlisted while awaiting valuation
  • Scaling issues: Peak seasons created bottlenecks and backlogs

Our Solution

We implemented an AI-powered property valuation system that combines machine learning with comprehensive market data to generate accurate valuations in minutes.

Key components:

  • Automated Data Aggregation: Pulls data from land registries, MLS systems, and public records
  • Comparable Analysis Engine: ML-powered identification and weighting of comparable sales
  • Market Trend Analysis: Real-time integration of market indicators and economic data
  • Property Feature Extraction: Computer vision analysis of listing photos for feature identification
  • Confidence Scoring: Transparency on valuation certainty with supporting data points

"We went from a 3-day valuation cycle to same-day listings. Our agents love it because properties hit the market faster, and our clients trust the pricing because it's data-driven."

— Head of Residential Sales

Implementation

The project was delivered in 14 weeks:

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Data integration and historical valuation analysis
  • Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): ML model training on 5+ years of transaction data
  • Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): User interface development and agent training
  • Phase 4 (Weeks 13-14): Parallel running and validation against manual valuations

The system was validated against 500 historical valuations, achieving 97% alignment with final sale prices.

Results

Six months after launch:

  • 90% faster valuations: From 2-3 days to under 30 minutes
  • 15% more accurate pricing: Properties selling closer to list price with less negotiation
  • CHF 180,000 annual savings: Reduced analyst time and faster property turnover
  • 25% increase in listings capacity: Agents can handle more properties
  • Standardized methodology: Consistent valuations across all properties and regions

Technology Stack

  • Secure cloud deployment with Swiss data residency
